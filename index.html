Frequently Asked Questions

What is Clout Clash? ▼

Clout Clash is a competitive social gacha battler built on Solana. Players collect NFT-based avatars representing different internet subcultures and battle in meme contests and mini-games to earn real SOL rewards.

How do I earn SOL? ▼

Earn SOL by engaging in social battles, meme contests, and community challenges. Your level of interaction—such as likes, reposts, and wins—directly contributes to your SOL rewards.

Do I need a crypto wallet? ▼

Yes. A Solana-compatible wallet such as Phantom is required to store your NFTs and earned SOL.

What are Clout Avatars? ▼

Clout Avatars are unique NFT characters inspired by various digital subcultures. They come with different traits and skills, and they can be collected, traded, and used to battle in the game.
